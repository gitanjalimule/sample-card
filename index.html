Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer known for his aggressive playstyle and consistent performance. He is a former captain of the Indian national team and has led India to many victories across all formats. Kohli has broken several records and is considered one of the greatest batsmen in modern cricket. He has received numerous awards including the ICC Cricketer of the Year and the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna. Off the field, he is also known for his fitness, brand endorsements, and charity work through the Virat Kohli Foundation.
